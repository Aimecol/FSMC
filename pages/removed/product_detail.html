Frequently Asked Questions

What is the warranty coverage for this GPS receiver?

The High-Precision GPS Receiver comes with a comprehensive 1-year manufacturer warranty that covers all hardware defects and normal wear and tear. We also offer extended warranty options that can provide coverage for up to 3 years. The warranty includes free firmware updates and technical support.

Is training included with the purchase?

Yes, your purchase includes a basic training package featuring video tutorials and a half-day online training session with one of our technical specialists. We also offer advanced training packages for an additional fee, which include comprehensive field training and customized workflow consultations.

Can this GPS receiver be integrated with third-party software?

Absolutely. The GPS receiver supports standard data formats including RINEX, NMEA, and various ASCII formats, making it compatible with most third-party surveying and GIS software. It also offers direct integration with popular platforms like AutoCAD, ArcGIS, and QGIS through our connection utilities, which are included in the software package.

What kind of accuracy can I expect in challenging environments?

In typical challenging environments such as urban canyons or under moderate tree canopy, you can expect horizontal accuracy of 1-3cm with RTK corrections. The advanced antenna design and tracking algorithms significantly improve performance in these conditions compared to standard receivers. For very challenging environments (dense forest or deep urban canyons), we recommend using our Multipath Reduction Module, available as an optional accessory.

Do you offer rental options for this equipment?

Yes, we offer flexible rental options for this GPS receiver. Our rental periods range from daily to monthly, with discounts available for longer-term rentals. This is an excellent way to test the equipment for your specific applications before making a purchase decision. Please contact our sales team for current rental rates and availability.
